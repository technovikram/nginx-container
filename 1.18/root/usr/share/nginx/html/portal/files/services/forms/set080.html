Visa Preauthorized Payment Cancellation
  The purpose of this form is to cancel preauthorized electronic funds transfers, such as automatic bill payments and installment payments. Please note: transactions that have already received an authorization approval must be allowed to clear and settle (PPCS will not stop these). For more information, please review Visa's Best Practices for the PPCS and the Visa Preauthorized Payment Cancellation Service quick reference.

You can use this form to request one of the following:
  • Stop payment order - stops a single specific preauthorized payment to a single merchant.
  • Revocation of authorization order - stops all future preauthorized payments to a particular merchant.
  • Revocation of all authorizations order - stops all bill payment transactions for a specific closed account.
