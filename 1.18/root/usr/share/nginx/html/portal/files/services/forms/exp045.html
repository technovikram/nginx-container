On the above holidays, and on any other holidays you designate, Card Services does not perform nightly processing for your organization. Instead, on the first working day after a holiday, your organization will receive:

  • One file containing reports for separate days (i.e., the day after a Monday holiday, the file will contain reports for Friday, Saturday, Sunday, and Monday).

  • Your AutoRecon or transaction posting files (if any), combined with the next working day's files.

If your organization will be closed and/or open on any weekday holidays besides the holidays listed above, please enter the dates in the text box below by December 5, 2013 (for 2014 holidays), after which a change fee of $250 applies. If your organization will not be closed on any additional days, no reply is needed.
